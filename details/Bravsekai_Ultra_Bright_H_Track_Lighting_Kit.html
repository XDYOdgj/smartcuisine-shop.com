Bravsekai Ultra-Bright H Type Track Lighting Kit

Transform your commercial space with bright, even illumination that's both stylish and functional. This 12-head track lighting system delivers powerful 2400lm of 6000K cool white light, offering a clean and vibrant ambiance for restaurants, offices, and more.

Bright & Even Illumination

The Bravsekai H Track Lighting Kit is designed to spread light evenly across large or small areas. Its 12 high-output LED spotlights provide consistent brightness and reduce shadows, making it perfect for commercial spaces that require uniform lighting.

Energy-Efficient & Long-Lasting

With 20W power and a 2400lm output, this track lighting system is built to be energy-efficient and save on electricity costs over time. The high-quality aluminum construction not only ensures durability but also allows for efficient heat dissipation, extending the lifespan up to 50,000 hours.

Customizable & Versatile

Whether you're looking for 6000K cool white or 3000K warm white lighting, the Bravsekai kit offers options to suit your specific needs. The dimmable plug-in feature simplifies installation and gives you control over brightness, making it adaptable for different lighting layouts and settings.

Durable & Stylish Design

Constructed from durable, lightweight aluminum, the track lighting kit not only stands the test of time but also blends seamlessly into modern interiors. Its sleek and user-friendly design makes it easy to install and maintain, ensuring year-round use without compromising on performance.
