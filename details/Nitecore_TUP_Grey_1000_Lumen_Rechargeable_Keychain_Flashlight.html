Nitecore TUP - The Ultimate Rechargeable Flashlight

A compact, high-performance light for everyday carry and any situation you might face.

The Nitecore TUP is a brilliant addition to your gear collection. Its impressive 1000 lumen output ensures you can light up large areas with ease, and the beam extends up to 197 yards for maximum reach. Designed for spontaneity and safety, it’s small enough to fit conveniently on your keychain or in your pocket—ideal for carrying with you wherever you go.

Whether you're hiking through the woods, driving at night, or just need a reliable light source in your bag, the TUP is built for performance and endurance. With a 1200mAh rechargeable battery, it offers up to 70 hours of runtime on the lowest setting. This makes it perfect for long trips or carrying through the day without worrying about running out of power.

A standout feature of the Nitecore TUP is its built-in OLED display. This allows you to keep track of essential details like brightness level, mode, lumen output, battery voltage, runtime, and lockout status—all at a glance. This ensures you’re always in control, even in the most challenging conditions.

The lockout mode is especially useful for travelers or those who need to keep the flashlight secured in their pockets. It prevents accidental activation, giving you peace of mind. This package comes with a LumenTac micro-USB charging cable, so you can charge it conveniently on the go without needing extra accessories.

Key Features

  • 1000 Lumen Output with 197 Yard Reach
  • 5 Brightness Modes for Versatile Use
  • USB Rechargeable with 70 Hour Runtime
  • OLED Display for Real-Time Monitoring
  • Lockout Mode for Safe Travel
