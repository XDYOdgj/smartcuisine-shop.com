Homevacious Antique Brass Sink Drain Stopper

The Homevacious Antique Brass Sink Drain Stopper brings a blend of classic elegance and modern convenience to your bathroom. With its rustproof finish, this drain stopper is built to last, maintaining its refined look through everyday use. The detachable strainer basket allows for effortless cleaning, while the intuitive push-to-close and push-to-open mechanism provides smooth water control with minimal effort. Made from solid all-metal construction, it ensures strength and reliability, making it an excellent choice for any bathroom sink setup.

Designed for easy installation, this drain stopper fits most standard US sink holes with openings ranging from 1-1/2" to 1-3/4". Its antique brass finish not only enhances the visual appeal of your bathroom but also resists scratches and tarnishing, keeping it looking new for years. The inclusion of all necessary gaskets and mounting hardware guarantees a leak-proof fit, making it the perfect solution for replacing your existing drain stopper or upgrading your bathroom with a stylish and functional addition.
