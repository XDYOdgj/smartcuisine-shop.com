Premium Brass Drain Plug for Bathrooms

Crafted from high-quality brass, this drain plug is built to last and deliver excellent performance in your bathroom. Whether you're using it for a sink, basin, or bathtub, its durable construction ensures long-term reliability and smooth operation. The specially designed threads make installation a breeze, while the spring mechanism guarantees effortless functionality every time.

This pop-up bolt is an ideal replacement for your existing basin waste plug. It features secure M8 top thread and M16 bottom thread connections, allowing for quick and easy assembly. With its simple screw-on and screw-off design, you can push the pop-up up and replace the waste plug without any hassle. A must-have for homeowners looking for a long-lasting, efficient solution to their drainage needs.
